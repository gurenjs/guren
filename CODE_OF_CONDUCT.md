# Code of Conduct

We are committed to providing a friendly, safe, and welcoming environment for everyone, regardless of background, identity, or experience level.

## Expected Behaviour

- Be respectful and inclusive in both language and actions.
- Assume positive intent, but recognise the impact of your words.
- Provide constructive feedback and be open to receiving it.
- Collaborate with empathy and patience.

## Unacceptable Behaviour

The following behaviours are not tolerated in any project spaces:

- Harassment, discrimination, or exclusionary jokes.
- Personal insults, especially those related to protected characteristics.
- Publishing others' private information without explicit permission.
- Sustained disruption of discussions, reviews, or community events.

## Reporting

If you experience or witness behaviour that violates this Code of Conduct, please report it to the maintainers via a private GitHub issue or discussion. All reports will be reviewed and investigated promptly and confidentially.

## Enforcement

Project maintainers may take action deemed appropriate for any unacceptable behaviour, including temporary or permanent bans from project spaces.
